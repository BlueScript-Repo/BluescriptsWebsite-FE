PECO Projects Pvt Ltd :

Project Description

1) Project & Inventry managment:- manages entire project through out the life cycle which include followings.
  a)BOQ/BOM Managment: Upload/create existing BOQ/BOM and maintain differnt versions of BOQ/BOM. Download any version on one click.
  b)Inquiry:- Generate & maintain inquiries sent to different vendors.Generate and Maintain enquiry by selecting items for BOQ/BOM.
  c)Purchase Order:- Generate and maintain PO on order in desired format for any inquiry. Generate a PO in Pdf/excel format. Ability to populate vendors details on one click. Maintain PO data related to each project. Access/download generate PO any time when required.
  d)Invoices :- Generate & Automatically sent invoices to different vendors and maintains them.Access/download invoices.
  e)Inventory:- Efficient way to manage project inventory to minimize the losses.
Tracking of the inventory throughout the project life cycle.
Maintaining the list of unused inventory with quantity, location & other details.
Exchanging inventory between project.
Generating chalan & invoices for purchased inventory.
